About Us

Home / About Us

Our Story

Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum is a dedicated team of volunteers committed to uniting and empowering the Slovak community in South Manchester and its surrounding areas. Founded in 2010, our charity has grown from a small group of passionate individuals to a thriving community hub.

We provide education to children and adults who wish to join the Slovak community and actively keep cultural traditions alive through various workshops and cultural events.

Our center serves as a bridge between traditional Slovak values and the modern multicultural environment of Manchester, creating a space where people can connect, learn, and celebrate their heritage.

Our Values

Community

We believe in the power of community to support, nurture, and elevate each member. Our center provides a welcoming space for people of all ages to connect and build meaningful relationships.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in all our programs and services, continuously improving and adapting to meet the needs of our community members and provide the highest quality educational resources.

Heritage

We are dedicated to preserving and celebrating Slovak heritage and cultural traditions, ensuring they continue to thrive and are passed down to future generations through education and cultural events.

Inclusivity

We welcome everyone interested in Slovak culture, regardless of background or origin. Our center is a place where diversity is celebrated and all are encouraged to participate and contribute.
